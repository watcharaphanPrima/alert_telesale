# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Sales Agents and their Managers. Agents use low-spec computers (Gen 8, 8GB RAM, no dedicated GPU) and need a fast way to request help. Managers need to monitor and respond to these alerts instantly.

## Product Purpose
A lightweight, real-time desktop SOS application. It allows telesales agents to request immediate assistance from their managers without disrupting their workflow, ensuring fast problem resolution.

## Brand Personality
Reliable, Fast, Friendly.

## Anti-references
Do not use excessive colors or heavy animations that would cause lag on low-spec PCs. Avoid complex nested cards or tiny unreadable text.

## Design Principles
1. Performance First: The UI must render instantly on low-spec machines.
2. Expert Confidence: The SOS action must feel solid and reliable, not flimsy.
3. Clarity over Decoration: Use visual cues (like color and size) for functional alerts, not just for aesthetics.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Ensure high contrast for readability. Keep motion minimal (respect prefers-reduced-motion) due to performance constraints and accessibility.
